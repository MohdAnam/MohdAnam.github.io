English Alphabet AR App

An Augmented Reality App which is based on English Alphabet and emerges 3D objects related to custom markers.

Children today can be taught better with immersive technology that is available, namely "Augmented Reality." This app helps children to use tablets or mobile phones on markers where objects emerge from the markers as children place the camera on it.

It creates a more engaging learning atmosphere. With such an ecosystem, students become more engaged in their learning, which will ultimately boost the number of students in school. It will help students to understand the shape and size of different things better and faster.

Features

There are three basic features implemented on the 3D models - phonic Sound, scaling, and rotation of the 3D models using touch gestures.
Scanning another marker. Rotating the 3D model using touch gestures.
